FAQ

Frequently asked
questions.

Is EarnHive free to join?
Yes! EarnHive is 100% free to create an account. You only need funds in your wallet to post tasks, apply for mini jobs or stake in games. There are no registration fees ever.
How do I deposit funds?
If you're in Nigeria, Ghana, Kenya or South Africa — deposit via Paystack using your bank card, bank transfer or USSD. For all other countries, send any cryptocurrency (TON, USDT, BTC etc.) via NOWPayments. Funds reflect instantly.
How do withdrawals work?
African users (Nigeria, Ghana, Kenya, South Africa) receive automatic bank transfers via Paystack. All other users receive TON or crypto automatically via NOWPayments API to any crypto wallet. Minimum withdrawal is $1 equivalent.
What is TON coin?
TON (The Open Network) is a fast, ultra-low-fee blockchain originally developed by Telegram. It's the native currency of EarnHive's global payment system. TON transactions confirm in seconds and cost less than $0.01 in fees — perfect for micro-payments.
How does the referral system work?
Invite friends using your unique referral link. For 7 days after they join, you automatically earn 10% of everything they earn from tasks. This comes from platform commission — no deduction from your friend's earnings. The more active friends you invite, the more you earn.
Is EarnHive available in my country?
Yes! EarnHive works in 100+ countries worldwide. If your country supports Paystack (Nigeria, Ghana, Kenya, South Africa) you get local bank payments. Everyone else can use crypto payments via NOWPayments — which works in virtually every country on earth.
What languages does EarnHive support?
EarnHive automatically detects your country and switches to your local language — including English, Hindi, Portuguese, French, Arabic, Spanish, Russian, Chinese, Swahili and many more. You can also manually select your preferred language in settings.
Are the games fair?
Yes. All game logic is transparent and fair. Ludo dice rolls use cryptographically random number generation. Whot card shuffles are genuinely random. No manipulation. The platform takes a flat 10% commission from every pot — that's our only income from games.
