How We Use Your Data
Section 2
  • Account access: Your email and password are used to let you log in securely to EarnHive.
  • Sending payments: Your TON wallet address is used only to send your earned crypto when you request a withdrawal. Nothing else.
  • Task tracking: We track which tasks you have completed, your submission history and your balance so the platform works correctly.
  • Notifications: We may send you important updates about your account, task approvals or platform changes by email.
  • Platform improvement: We use general activity data to understand how the platform is used and improve the experience for everyone.
  • Fraud prevention: We monitor activity to detect fake submissions, duplicate accounts and cheating to protect honest earners and advertisers.
Who We Share Your Data With
Section 3
We never sell your personal data to anyone. We do not share your data with advertisers, third-party marketers or data brokers. Your information is yours.
  • Firebase by Google: Handles your login and authentication securely. Your password is never stored in plain text — Firebase encrypts it.
  • Supabase: Stores your profile, task history and submissions. Data is stored on secure servers with encryption.
  • Legal requirements: If a court of law or government authority legally requires us to share data, we are obligated to comply. We will notify you if possible.
  • Never shared: Your wallet address, phone number and personal details are never shared with advertisers or any third parties for marketing.

Cookies and Local Storage
Section 5
  • Login session: We use Firebase session cookies to keep you logged in. Without this cookie, you would have to log in every time you visit.
  • Preferences: We may store your language or display preferences locally in your browser so the site works better for you.
  • No advertising cookies: EarnHive does not use tracking cookies for advertising. We do not show ads and we do not track you across other websites.
  • You can clear cookies and local storage at any time from your browser settings. This will log you out of EarnHive.

Young Users and Teenagers
Section 7
EarnHive welcomes young earners. We believe teenagers deserve the opportunity to earn money for themselves — for school supplies, data, or personal needs. There is no minimum age to join as an earner.
  • We take extra care to protect the data of young users. We collect only what is necessary and never share it with third parties.
  • Young users will never see inappropriate tasks. All task content is reviewed by admin before going live on the platform.
  • If you are a parent concerned about your child's account on EarnHive, please contact us and we will assist you immediately.
  • Advertisers (task posters) must be 18 or older. This protects young earners from being exploited by unverified task creators.
